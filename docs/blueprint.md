## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Offer: Displays the main offer, including headline, subheadline, price, and a primary CTA to the checkout page.
- Recipe Preview Section: Presents a list of recipe highlights to showcase the variety and simplicity of the recipes included in the product.
- Offer Card with Testimonials: Highlights the promotional value with a visually distinct card, combined with social proof from user testimonials.
- FAQ Section: Addresses common customer queries, providing essential information about the product and its use.
- Sticky Footer CTA: A persistent call-to-action bar at the bottom of the screen on mobile devices, encouraging immediate purchase.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Green (#16A34A) for emphasis on key elements like price and CTAs, reflecting freshness and value. 
- Background color: Dark gray (#0A0A0A) for a modern, high-contrast feel that makes the content stand out.
- Accent color: Subtle yellow border in offer card (#FFC857, approximately) to add a touch of warmth and attract attention to the discount.
- Font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for both headlines and body text to maintain readability and a modern, clean aesthetic throughout the landing page.
- Mobile-first layout with a clean, single-column structure on smaller screens that transitions to a more structured multi-column layout on larger screens to ensure an optimal viewing experience on any device.
- Subtle hover animations to enhance interactivity (e.g., a slight opacity change or scale on buttons).
